2 BR, 1 Bath in
Central Brooklyn

  • Description

    This ground-floor 2 bedroom apartment in Central Brooklyn is minutes aways from Prospect Park, the Brooklyn Museum and several subway lines, in a neighborhood that saw a 23% decrease in the number of Black residents from from 2000 to 2015.

    This owner is choosing to keep their unit vacant because they are actively taking part in the long history of racial exclusion, redlining, theft, segregation, displacement, and inequality perpetrated against the Black residents of Crown Heights by tools such as deed theft, speculative real estate purchasing, racist real estate laws, and police violence.

  • Cost

    The legal rent is $844.

    Market rate rent is $2,400

    The cost of racist displacement in Crown Heights is the loss of community, an increase in poverty and homelessness, and growing incentives for landlords to engage in horrific tenant harrassment.
